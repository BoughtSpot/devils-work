Synopsis

After the murder of Judy Laing in her ofice, her children take over the operations of her FEMME NGO as written in her will, but they must also help the police ind her killer whiles dealing with their own personal demons.

Character Profiles

Judy Laing

Judy Laing is murdered at age 62, before that she run a strictly all women’s NGO catering for the needs of abused women in the Country. Judy also has a colorful past which starts to unravel once her children take over the running of the company and realize that their mother kept a lot of secrets from them, one of which could lead them to her murderer.

Maxine

36 year old Maxine is the eldest of three children and the new CEO of an NGO organization for women which her mother used to own and run until she was murdered by what police investigators are saying could have been committed by one of her female clients. In the mist of the storm Maxine must find the Strength to keep the peace among his siblings, sustain her mother’s business whiles dealing with the demons of her own marriage.

Chantel

29 year old Chantel grew up going against the norm and so was constantly in trouble with authorities and especially her mother. Having lost the support of her mother at an early age Chantel learnt to look after herself the way she knew how and after the death of her mother and her sudden inheritance of money, she’s not about to go easy on life, in fact she thinks it’s her time to shine, prove herself and get everything she never had with her mother alive. But beneath the ‘I don’t care’ exterior lies a very lonely person yearning for a lot of attention and love.

Jude

27 year old Jude, the last of the siblings and seemingly the less burdened runs a modelling agency and small fashion business. His affiliation with young women makes him popular with them and gives an edge over his siblings when it comes to dealing with the women clients. But not every woman has good intentions, especially those that want more from their not so colorful lives and Jude may just be the pawn they need to get what they really want.

Isaac

31 year old Isaac is the other child who only appears after the burial of the dead woman and claims his spot as the fourth child of Judy Laing. According to Isaac he was born in a village in Tarkwa where Judy had practiced as a nurse for two years. He was given up for adoption and reunited with Judy a few years before her death. Isaac is the misfit of the siblings, he’s the awkward one but he’s also the one that seems to know their mother’s darkest secrets and possibly the murderer because he did the dirty work for her on behalf of her clients.

Laura

25 year old Laura has worked with the murdered woman for close to five years. She’s dependable and efficient but hasn’t lost sight of the fact that the children of her late boss have come into money therefore playing her cards well could make her part of this family for good which will inadvertently enhance her not so colorful life.

Detective Mensah

36 year old Detective Mensah is efficient and hardworking but he’s the kind who will loosen the cuffs to allow the work to be done. He’s also the guy looking for his soul mate but his desire to solve a murder and find love will come at a very high price.

Peter

38 year old Peter is a desperate man who is trying to save his dying marriage to Maxine. He’s also wheel chair bound and dependent on the affection of a woman who can’t seem to forgive his past.
